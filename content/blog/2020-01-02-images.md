You can put images in your markdown and they are nicely sized, with all sorts of tweaks for publishing.

Code like:
~~~markdown
![white worms](images/hugh-grant-and-pal.jpg)
~~~

Results in:

![white worms](images/hugh-grant-and-pal.jpg)
